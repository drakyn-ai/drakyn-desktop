How many learning questions agent can ask per day
How It Works

The agent monitors:

  • Your emails (via Gmail)
  • System state (battery, etc.)
  • Calendar events (when implemented)

It suggests helpful actions like:

  • Reminders about important emails
  • Charging your laptop when battery is low
  • Preparing for upcoming meetings

Privacy: All monitoring happens locally. No data leaves your machine.
